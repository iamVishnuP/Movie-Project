# Welcome to Cinema Kottakam: The Cinephile’s Universe

Cinema Kottakam is not just another movie app—it is a premium community designed for those who live and breathe cinema. Built on the principles of discovery, discussion, and deep connection, it transforms your movie-watching experience into a social journey.

---

## 🎬 1. Your Onboarding: The Stage is Set
When you join our "Kottakam" (Theater), you don't just create an account—you step into a cinematic identity.
- **Character Name**: Choose a unique character name that defines your persona in the cinematic world.
- **Preferences**: Select your favorite genres, preferred languages, and iconic directors. This forms the foundation of our **Discovery Engine**.

## 🚀 2. The Discovery Engine
Stop scrolling endlessly. Our engine analyzes your "Watched" history and "Watchlist" to recommend films that resonate with your specific taste.
- **Upcoming**: Track the most anticipated future releases.
- **Now Playing**: See what's currently shaking the theaters.
- **Personalized Rows**: Discover unique rows curated specifically for your tastes.

## 👥 3. Building Your Circle
Cinema is a shared experience.
- **Find People**: Search for other cinephiles by their character names or real names.
- **Connections**: Send connection requests to build your "Cinephile Circle."
- **Profiles**: View others' collections to see their favorite directors and movies, gaining inspiration for your next watch.

## 💬 4. Deep-Threaded Discussions
This is the heart of Cinema Kottakam. Create private "Rooms" focused on specific movies to dive deep into analysis.
- **Start a Room**: Select a movie, write a short caption, and provide your detailed "First Thoughts."
- **Invites**: Invite your connections to join the room.
- **Real-Time Analysis**: Post your thoughts, theories, and reactions.
- **Visual Evidence**: Upload images directly from your device to support your analysis or share iconic moments.
- **Reactions**: React to others' posts with a curated set of cinematic emojis.

## 📑 5. Library Management
Keep your collection organized.
- **Watchlist**: Save films you plan to watch.
- **Already Watched**: Mark films as watched and give them a star rating.
- **State Sync**: Your library is synced across the entire site. If you change a status in your library, it reflects everywhere—on the home page, search results, and discussion rooms.

---

### Tips for a Premium Experience
- **Auto-Sync**: Use the site and refresh with confidence; your session and state are managed with maximum stability.
- **Mobile First**: Add the site to your mobile home screen for a native-app feel with real-time notifications.
- **Be Bold**: Start discussions! The best cinematic insights often come from the most unexpected comparisons.

**Enjoy the show.**
